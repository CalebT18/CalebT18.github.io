The Hunter Path

You are walking through the woods when you spot someone — Bruised, panicked, running like their life depends on it They trip, look back, and lock eyes with you. Yeah, they’re definitely being hunted. And now they’re looking at you like you’re their only chance. So… what do you do? Do you help them? Or Do you catch them before someone else does? → Help the hunted — go right. → Catch them — go left. Heads up: Helping might just keep you alive. But catching? Let’s just say… not all hunters make it back.
